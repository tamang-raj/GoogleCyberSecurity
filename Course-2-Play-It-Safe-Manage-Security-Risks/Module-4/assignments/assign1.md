# Key Concepts Learned

## What is a Playbook?
A playbook is a documented guide that outlines standardized procedures for responding to security incidents consistently and efficiently.

## Six Phases of an Incident Response Playbook

1. **Preparation**
   - Develop procedures
   - Train employees
   - Prepare tools and resources

2. **Detection and Analysis**
   - Detect suspicious activity
   - Analyze incidents
   - Determine impact

3. **Containment**
   - Limit damage
   - Prevent further spread

4. **Eradication and Recovery**
   - Remove threats
   - Restore systems safely

5. **Post-Incident Activity**
   - Document lessons learned
   - Improve playbooks
   - Strengthen defenses

6. **Coordination**
   - Share information
   - Communicate with stakeholders
   - Ensure compliance throughout the response process
