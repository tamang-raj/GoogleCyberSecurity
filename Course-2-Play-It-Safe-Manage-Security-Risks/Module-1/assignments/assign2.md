# Question 1 ✅

### Question
**What is a vulnerability?**

### Correct Answer
- **A weakness that can be exploited by a threat**

### Explanation
A **vulnerability** is a weakness in a system, application, process, or person that a threat actor can exploit to compromise security.

**Examples:**
- Weak passwords
- Unpatched software
- Misconfigured systems
- Human error

# Question 2 ✅

### Question
**Fill in the blank:**  
Information protected by regulations or laws is a _____.

### Correct Answer
- **High-risk asset**

### Explanation
A **high-risk asset** is information that, if compromised, could seriously impact an organization's:

- Finances
- Operations
- Reputation
- Legal compliance

**Examples include:**
- Personally Identifiable Information (PII)
- Protected Health Information (PHI)
- Financial records
- Customer data

### Correct Answers

### Explanation

# Question 4 ✅

### Question
**Fill in the blank:**  
The steps in the Risk Management Framework (RMF) are prepare, _____, select, implement, assess, authorize, and monitor.

### Correct Answer
- **Categorize**

### Explanation

The **NIST Risk Management Framework (RMF)** consists of seven steps:

1. Prepare
2. Categorize
3. Select
4. Implement
5. Assess
6. Authorize
7. Monitor

The **Categorize** step identifies the security impact level of information systems based on the **CIA Triad** (Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability).
