# Quiz Results

## Question 1

### Question
**A security team wants some of its services to be hosted on the internet instead of local devices. However, they also need to maintain physical control over certain confidential data. What type of SIEM solution should they select?**

- Remote
- Cloud-hosted
- Self-hosted
- ✅ **Hybrid**

### ✔ Correct Answer
**Hybrid**

### Explanation
A **Hybrid SIEM** combines both **cloud-hosted** and **self-hosted** environments.

It allows organizations to:

- Keep sensitive or confidential data on local infrastructure.
- Take advantage of cloud services for scalability and flexibility.
- Balance security, performance, and cost.

This is ideal for organizations that need both cloud capabilities and physical control over critical assets.

## Question 2

### Question
**Security Information and Event Management (SIEM) tools provide dashboards that help cybersecurity professionals organize and focus their security efforts.**

- ✅ **True**
- False

### ✔ Correct Answer
**True**

### Explanation
SIEM dashboards provide visual representations of security data, helping analysts:

- Monitor threats in real time.
- Prioritize high-risk events.
- Analyze trends.
- Investigate incidents quickly.
- Improve overall incident response.

Dashboards reduce the need to manually review thousands of log entries.

## Question 3

### Question
**Fill in the blank: A _____ SIEM tool is specifically designed to take advantage of cloud computing capabilities including availability, flexibility, and scalability.**

- cloud-hardware
- ✅ **cloud-native**
- cloud-infrastructure
- cloud-local

### ✔ Correct Answer
**Cloud-native**

### Explanation
A **cloud-native SIEM** is built specifically for cloud environments rather than being adapted from traditional on-premises systems.

Benefits include:

- High scalability
- Greater flexibility
- Better availability
- Faster deployment
- Automatic updates
- Efficient handling of large volumes of log data

**Example:** Google **Chronicle**.

## Question 4

### Question
**What are the different types of SIEM tools? (Select three)**

- ✅ **Self-hosted**
- ✅ **Cloud-hosted**
- ✅ **Hybrid**
- Physical

### ✔ Correct Answers

- Self-hosted
- Cloud-hosted
- Hybrid

### Explanation

### Self-hosted SIEM
- Installed and maintained by the organization.
- Provides maximum control over data and infrastructure.

### Cloud-hosted SIEM
- Managed by a service provider.
- Accessible over the internet with minimal maintenance.

### Hybrid SIEM
- Combines self-hosted and cloud-hosted solutions.
- Offers flexibility while keeping sensitive data under local control.

**"Physical"** is **not** a type of SIEM deployment.
