### Correct Answer

# Question 2

### Question

**Competitor organizations are the biggest threat to a company’s security.**

### Correct Answer

❌ **False**

### Explanation

People are the biggest threat to an organization's security. Human error, phishing attacks, social engineering, and insider threats are common causes of security incidents. This is why employee security awareness and training are essential.

# Question 3

### Question

**Fill in the blank:**  
Security controls are safeguards designed to reduce _____ security risks.

### Correct Answer

✅ **specific**

### Completed Sentence

> Security controls are safeguards designed to reduce **specific** security risks.

### Explanation

Security controls are implemented to address particular risks, such as unauthorized access, malware infections, or data breaches.

# Question 4

### Question

A security analyst works on a project designed to reduce the risk of **vishing**. They develop a plan to protect their organization from attackers who could exploit biometrics.

**Which type of security control does this scenario describe?**

### Correct Answer

✅ **Authentication**

### Explanation

Authentication verifies the identity of a user or system before granting access. Examples include:

- Passwords
- Fingerprint recognition
- Face recognition
- Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA)
- Security keys
