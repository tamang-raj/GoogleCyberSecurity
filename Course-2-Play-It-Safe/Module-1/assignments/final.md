# Question 8 ❌

### Question
**Which of the following statements accurately describe risk?** *(Select all that apply.)*

### Your Answers
- ✅ Another way to think of risk is the likelihood of a threat occurring.
- ✅ A medium-risk asset may cause some damage to an organization's ongoing operations.

### ❌ Missing Correct Answer
- ✅ **A high-risk asset is any information protected by regulations or laws.**

### Explanation
Risk refers to anything that can impact the **Confidentiality, Integrity, or Availability (CIA)** of an asset.

Asset classifications include:
- **High-risk assets:** Protected by laws or regulations (PII, PHI, financial records)
- **Medium-risk assets:** May impact operations if compromised
- **Low-risk assets:** Cause minimal damage if compromised

# Question 10 ✅

### Question
**In the Risk Management Framework (RMF), which step involves officially approving a system to operate and taking responsibility for its potential risks?**

### Correct Answer
- **Authorize**

### Explanation
During the **Authorize** step, management reviews assessment results, accepts residual risk, and formally approves the system for operation.
