## Question 1

### Fill in the blank:

Cybersecurity is the practice of ensuring _____ by protecting networks, devices, people, and data from unauthorized access or criminal exploitation.

### Correct Answer

✅ **Confidentiality, Integrity, and Availability of information**

### Explanation

Cybersecurity focuses on protecting information based on the **CIA Triad**:

- **Confidentiality** – Prevent unauthorized access to information.
- **Integrity** – Ensure information remains accurate and unaltered.
- **Availability** – Ensure authorized users can access information when needed.

## Question 2

### What are the primary responsibilities of an entry-level security analyst?

**Select three answers.**

### Correct Answers

- ✅ Protect information
- ✅ Monitor systems
- ✅ Search for weaknesses

### Explanation

Entry-level cybersecurity analysts are responsible for:

- Monitoring networks and systems for suspicious activity.
- Protecting sensitive information from cyber threats.
- Identifying vulnerabilities before attackers can exploit them.

## Question 3

### Fill in the blank:

Performing _____ enables security professionals to review an organization's security records, activities, and related documents.

### Correct Answer

✅ **Security audits**

### Explanation

A **security audit** is a systematic review of an organization's:

- Security policies
- Procedures
- System configurations
- Access controls
- Security records
- Compliance with regulations

Its goal is to identify weaknesses and improve an organization's overall security posture.

## Question 4

### In what ways do security teams bring value to an organization?

**Select two answers.**

### Correct Answers

- ✅ Achieving regulatory compliance
- ✅ Protecting against external and internal threats

### Explanation

Security teams help organizations by:

- Protecting systems, networks, and data from cyber threats.
- Ensuring compliance with laws, regulations, and industry standards.
- Reducing business risks.
- Maintaining customer trust.
- Supporting business continuity.
