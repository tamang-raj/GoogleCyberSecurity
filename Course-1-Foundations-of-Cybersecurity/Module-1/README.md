## 📖 Overview

This repository contains my notes, learning progress, and achievements for **Module 1** of the **Google Cybersecurity Professional Certificate** offered by Google on Coursera.

This module introduces the fundamentals of cybersecurity, explores common security concepts, explains the responsibilities of cybersecurity professionals, and discusses the essential technical and workplace skills needed for an entry-level cybersecurity career.

---

## 🎯 Learning Objectives

By completing this module, I learned to:

- Understand what cybersecurity is and why it is important.
- Identify common cyber threats and attacks.
- Recognize the responsibilities of an entry-level cybersecurity analyst.
- Understand common cybersecurity terminology.
- Learn about the career paths within cybersecurity.
- Identify technical and transferable skills required in cybersecurity.
- Understand how cybersecurity protects organizations, users, and data.
- Recognize phishing attacks and common social engineering techniques.
